Biography

Professional DJ since 1996, Deejay VAX started at the age of 16 in Paris clubs with house music. He organized numerous parties in France and was a resident in several clubs, including mixing at the Gay Pride in Paris. After his time in Heidelberg (DE) where he performed in different clubs, he settled near Basel (CH) over 15 years ago, where he continues to produce and mix on different radios, clubs, bars. He has notably performed at Extrema Outdoor in Belgium and Nature One Camp. He enjoys producing and mixing different genres including Deep House, Tech House, Techno, Uplift Trance.
