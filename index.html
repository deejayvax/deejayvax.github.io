Biography

Professional DJ since 1996, Deejay VAX started at the age of 16 in Parisian clubs with funk, disco, R&B, and house music. He organized numerous parties in France and was a resident in several clubs in Paris, including mixing at the Gay Pride in Paris. After his time in Germany at Heidelberg where he performed in different clubs, he settled near Basel over 15 years ago, where he continues to produce and mix on different radios, clubs, bars. He has notably performed at Extrema Outdoor in Belgium and Nature One Camp. He enjoys mixing different genres including Afro, Deep House, Tech House, Techno.
